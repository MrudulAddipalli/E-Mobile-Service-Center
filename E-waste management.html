E-Waste Management

E Mobile Service Center is proud to be a part of your lives but we also have another very important part to play. As a responsible organization, Karbonn recognizes the importance of safe disposal of electronic waste. In view of the legislation called E-Waste (Management and Handling) Rules, 2011, passed by the Ministry of Environment & Forests (MoEF) and which has been in effect from May 1, 2012; we have been actively working towards the cause.

In addition to complying with all the applicable laws related to E-Waste management, we also seek support and cooperation from our customers in order to reduce the environmental side effects of electronic waste. To take the efforts a level higher, we have partnered with an e-waste company called 3R Recycler. The company collects, dismantles and disposes e-waste from around the country. 3R also believes in complete recycling of e-waste to turn it into an important resource.

To know more about our e-waste recycling initiative or our partnership with 3R Recycler, log on to www.3rrecycler.com or write an email to mruduladdipalli@gmail.com
